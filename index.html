About Us

Why Reed's Chocolate

We are a family endeavor devoted to making premium quality chocolate and cocoa items since 2000.The craft of making chocolate is one that is carefully and affectionately culminated after some time. Furthermore, our image ‘Reed's Chocolate’ is an impression of our energy and love that goes into making fine chocolate

At ‘Reed's Chocolate’ , we have faith in unadulterated chocolate that is wealthy in flavor and free of destructive Trans fats. We make a scope of unadulterated chocolate ‘Reed's Chocolate’ containing 52%, 70% and 85% cocoa. This ensures ‘Reed's Chocolate’ chocolate has the brilliant regular smell, surface and kind of cocoa, while holding all the medical advantages of cocoa, which are generally lost.
